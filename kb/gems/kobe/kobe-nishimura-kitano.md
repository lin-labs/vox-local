A 1948 members-club-turned-café in an ivy-covered Kitano house — Kobe's oldest kissaten, where locals linger over hand-drip and thick-cut morning toast.

## Details
Open 08:30–22:00 daily. The Kitanozaka branch (not the Sannomiya one) has the fireplace room upstairs. Morning set until 11:00 is the move: drip coffee + toast + soft egg.

## Insider notes
Ask for the second-floor window table overlooking the slope. Cash preferred. Five minutes' walk from the Kitano ijinkan houses, so pair it with an early stroll before the tour buses arrive at 10.
