An eight-seat teppan counter two alleys off Ikuta shrine where the chef buys one whole A5 Tajima carcass a month and serves cuts you will never see on a menu — the real Kobe beef experience without the hotel-restaurant markup.

## Details
Dinner seatings 18:00 and 20:30. Course from ¥14,000; the "chef's cut omakase" adds misuji and zabuton. Closed Wednesdays.

## Insider notes
Phone booking only, Japanese preferred — this is exactly the kind of call the concierge voice line should make for the guest. Say you want the counter, not the back table. If they're full, ask about the 20:30 second seating; cancellations cluster there.
