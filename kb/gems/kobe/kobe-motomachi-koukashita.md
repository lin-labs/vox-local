The brick arches under the JR tracks between Motomachi and Kobe stations hide a strip of six-seat standing bars and retro izakaya where salarymen drink Nada-district sake by the glass — zero tourists, maximum Kobe.

## Details
Best 18:00–22:00; most stalls close by 23:00. Start at the east end (Motomachi side) and drift west. Budget ¥3,000–4,000 for a three-stop crawl.

## Insider notes
Nada, twenty minutes east, is Japan's biggest sake-brewing district — if the guest likes what they drink here, book the Hakutsuru or Kiku-Masamune brewery tasting for the next morning.
