Arima's famous iron-red "gold water" public bath is a zoo by mid-morning — but at the 08:00 opening it's just you, the steam, and a few grandmothers who've been coming for fifty years.

## Details
Open 08:00–22:00, closed 2nd & 4th Tuesday. ¥800 entry; towels rentable. The kinsen (gold) water is scalding in the deep tub — the shallow one runs about 42°C.

## Insider notes
Guests staying at ryokan with only silver-water baths should do this at opening, then breakfast after. There's a free kinsen foot bath just outside for anyone who won't do the full public bath.
