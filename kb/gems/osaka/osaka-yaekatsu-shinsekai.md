The kushikatsu counter the Janjan-yokocho locals actually queue at — thinner batter and fresher oil than the tourist giants, with the no-double-dip sauce trough done right.

## Details
Roughly 10:30–20:30, closed Thursdays. Order in rounds of three skewers; beef, renkon, and shiso-pork first. Cash only.

## Insider notes
If the line is long, the doteyaki (miso-stewed beef tendon) stall two doors down is the perfect holding pattern. Fifteen minutes from Namba on the Midosuji line — good evening add-on to a Kobe-based trip.
