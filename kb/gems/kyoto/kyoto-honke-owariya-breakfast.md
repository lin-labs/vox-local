A soba house founded in 1465 — older than Columbus — that fed Kyoto's temples and the imperial palace; arrive at the 11:00 opening and eat hourai soba in a creaking machiya before the queue forms.

## Details
11:00–15:30, closed Jan 1–2. Hourai soba (five-tier tower with eight toppings) is the signature; the sobayu comes automatically at the end.

## Insider notes
The main honten store, not the department-store branches. After 11:45 the line runs 45 minutes; at open you walk straight in. Vegetarians: order the herring-free nishin alternatives — broth contains bonito unless you ask for the shojin (temple) option a day ahead by phone.
